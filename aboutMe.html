About me

I've always had a creative mindset and a strong interest in technology. Learning how different tools and programs work has always come naturally to me, which sparked my desire to build a career in the digital world.
During high school (HTX), I was introduced to programming with Python and C++, as well as web design using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I quickly fell in love with frontend development, especially the visual and interactive aspects of creating websites.
After graduating, I began studying Game Development and Learning Technology at SDU. Here, I worked with C# in Unity, Java, MongoDB, and R, and I gained a solid understanding of design theory, particularly in the context of game development. While it was a valuable experience, I realized that my true passion lies in web design, so I chose to change direction.
I'm now studying Multimedia Design at UCL, where I can focus more deeply on what I enjoy most: designing and building websites. I love working with frontend code to bring hi-fi prototypes and mockups to life, whether it's through experimenting with color schemes, implementing responsive designs, or solving design problems with creative code solutions.
