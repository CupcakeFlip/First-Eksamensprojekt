Projects

Here you can explore a selection of my past projects to get a better sense of my skills, interests, and what I'm capable of creating.

Dating app for dogs

This project is a high-fidelity prototype for a fictional dating app for dogs, designed in Figma. It demonstrates my ability to create intuitive UI layouts and apply visual design principles to a fun, imaginative concept.

Website for danish mastiff club

I was assigned to design the website for Dansk Mastiff Klub. The project is currently in the early stages, and the Figma wireframes you see here reflect the initial design direction. I'm currently awaiting feedback before moving forward with development.

Moving art

As part of a workshop, we were tasked with animating a static painting. I chose "The Bachelor Party" by Louis Wain and created a short animation to bring the piece to life. This exercise gave me the chance to work creatively with motion and storytelling through visuals.

Interactive story in Unity

In this project, I created an interactive story experience using Unity. Although I didn't create the animations myself, I was responsible for assembling the project, making it interactive, and building the logic behind the experience. Note: The story is in Danish, and it's only available as a Windows download due to compatibility limitations.

SunMood - Weather & Mood Tracker

This website was created in 2023 as part of a project that required API integration. Using a weather API, I built a page that displays the current weather and compares today's sunshine hours with yesterday's. The idea behind SunMood was to help people affected by seasonal depression track their mood alongside sunlight exposure. The plan was to allow users to log their mood, enabling them to see trends over time based on sun levels.
